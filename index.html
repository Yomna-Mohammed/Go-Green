How to plant plants

The plants need :
  • Large pot for the plant
  • Suitable soil for the plant
  • Water daily
  • Sunny place
  • Caring for the plant

Types of plants

Shrubs
Shrubs are smaller than trees, with multiple stems.
Herbs
Herbs are non-woody plants, often short and making persistent above-ground stems.
Trees
Trees are large, woody plants with a main trunk and branches.
Climbers
Climbers are plants with long, weak stems that need support to grow vertically, for example, Vines.
Aquatic plants
Aquatic plants are plants growing in water or saturated soil.
Grasses
Grasses are herbaceous plants with narrow leaves and jointed stems.
